Question

What are the main components of a plant cell that distinguish it from an animal cell?

Tap to reveal answer

Answer

Plant cells have three main distinguishing components:

  • Cell Wall: Rigid structure providing support and protection
  • Chloroplasts: Organelles containing chlorophyll for photosynthesis
  • Large Central Vacuole: Storage compartment maintaining turgor pressure
